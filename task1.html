Ministry of Education and Higher Education (MOEHE)

Strategy / Design / Code

Brief

Ministry of Education and Higher Education (MOEHE) Qatar. Education is one of the most critical pillars in Qatar's vision of a diversified, knowledge-based economy and a prosperous future for its citizens. The Ministry's goal is to produce confident, self-reliant and hard-working critical thinkers. Students will graduate with 21stcentury skills and values to succeed in higher education, in the workforce and in life.

Objective

Solution

The solution involved creating a portal accessible by multiple internal teams, stakeholders and the public.

Mobile APP
